R2 Clouder — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

R2 Clouder is a macOS desktop application for managing Cloudflare R2 cloud storage. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how R2 Clouder handles your data.

Data Collection

R2 Clouder does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app operates entirely on your local device.

Credentials Storage

Your Cloudflare R2 API credentials (Access Key ID and Secret Access Key) are stored locally on your Mac using AES-256-GCM encryption. Encryption keys are stored in the macOS Keychain and never leave your device.

Network Communication

R2 Clouder communicates directly with Cloudflare R2 endpoints (S3-compatible API) using your provided credentials. No data is routed through any third-party servers. No intermediary services are involved.

Analytics and Tracking

R2 Clouder contains no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no advertising. The app does not use any third-party analytics frameworks.

Third-Party Services

The only external service R2 Clouder connects to is Cloudflare R2, at your explicit direction. No other third-party services are used.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at dan@danburian.dev.
