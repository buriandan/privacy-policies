DO Deployer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

DO Deployer is a macOS menu bar application for monitoring DigitalOcean App Platform deployments. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

DO Deployer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app operates entirely on your local device.

Credentials Storage

Your DigitalOcean API token is stored securely in the macOS Keychain. The token never leaves your device except when making authenticated API requests to DigitalOcean.

Network Communication

DO Deployer communicates directly with the DigitalOcean API (api.digitalocean.com) to fetch deployment status and health metrics. No data is routed through any third-party servers.

Local Data

Deployment event logs are stored locally in the app's sandboxed container on your Mac. These logs are not transmitted anywhere and remain under your control.

Analytics and Tracking

DO Deployer contains no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no advertising.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at dan@danburian.dev.
